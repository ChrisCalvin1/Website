About Me

Jaxon Maverick

I’m Jaxon Maverick, a 17-year-old fitness and coding enthusiast from Colombo, Sri Lanka. My passion for weightlifting and sports has shaped who I am. Recently, I also discovered the world of coding, which excites me because of its blend of logic and creativity.

I’ve been learning Python, JavaScript, and HTML/CSS. I enjoy how a few lines of code can create something powerful — from websites to games. Coding feels like an art form, just like fitness does to me.
